# Early Contributors

**Thomas W. Messerer**
*Co Founder & CEO*

Thomas is a passionate entrepreneur and was very early in the DePIN space. Since fully committing to Web3 in 2019, he co-founded, alongside his brothers, one of the largest companies specializing in deployment-as-a-service for first-generation DePINs across major European cities. The invaluable insights he gained during those formative years laid the foundation for Silencio, combining innovative thinking with practical solutions to address real-world challenges fairly and efficiently.

Before entering the blockchain industry, Thomas, alongside his brother Theo, co-managed a team of over 100 professionals as Partners at Moeller IP Advisors. Together, they guided Fortune 500 companies in managing Intellectual Property portfolios across emerging markets.

Thomas's academic journey includes a Bachelor's degree in Economics and Finance from Bocconi University and a Master's in Management from IE University. His early interest in blockchain led him to complete the Blockchain Strategy Program at the Saïd Business School, University of Oxford, in 2016.

Outside of his professional pursuits, Thomas is an adventurer at heart. He draws inspiration from mountaineering, self-sufficiency, and challenging expeditions that test his physical and mental limits. He is a lifelong learner.

**Eric Stotz**
*Co Founder & CDO*

Eric Stotz, a visionary entrepreneur, is the co-founder of Buenbit, a leading crypto exchange platform operating in Argentina, Mexico, and Peru. The platform boasts 150 employees and over 1 million users.

An accomplished industrial engineer, Eric earned his degree from Universidad Catolica Argentina. He brings a wealth of experience, having spent four years at Bain & Company, where he collaborated with top management of large national and multinational groups across various industries, including Financial Services, Automotive, Mining, Oil & Gas, Steel, Insurance, and Education. Eric has actively participated in the blockchain space since 2017, continually pushing the boundaries of innovation.

In his leisure time, Eric enjoys golfing, competing in triathlons, and expressing his artistic side through playing the piano.
