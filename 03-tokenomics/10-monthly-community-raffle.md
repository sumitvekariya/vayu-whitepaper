# Monthly Community Raffle

Silencio's Deep In Luck – A Monthly Blockchain Raffle System

## Unlock Your $SLC Rewards with the "Deep in Luck" Raffle!

Join the **"Deep in Luck" Raffle**, where over **100 million unique prizes** worth more than **500 million $SLC tokens** are up for grabs every single month! Claim your mystery cubes and discover what surprises the Silencio universe has in store for you.

## How It Works

1.  **Collect Raffle Tickets:** Earn in-app Coins by measuring noise levels with the app or inviting friends. Each Coin earned equals one raffle ticket.
2.  **Increase Your Chances:** The more tickets you collect, the higher your chances of winning valuable mystery cubes and growing your $SLC rewards.
3.  **Claim Your Prizes:** Starting the first week of each month, you'll have **21 days** to open your mystery cubes and claim your $SLC prizes from the previous month.

Silencio distributes 541,666,666.67 $SLC tokens from the community reserve each month to reward community contributions. These tokens are allocated through a raffle system automatically including all active users.

## Epic Prizes Await

With **ten prize categories**, rewards range from **Iron Nuggets worth 1 $SLC** to **Diamonds worth over 11 million $SLC!** The more tickets you have, the better your odds of hitting the jackpot.

## Boost Your Rewards with the OG Bonus

Get an extra **20% bonus** on all Coins or tickets you've earned in the past year—both from your own activity and referrals. The OG bonus significantly increases your chances of winning bigger rewards!

## Stake and Level Up

Take your participation to the next level by staking your $SLC raffle winnings. Staking boosts your OG bonus for future raffles and unlocks exclusive perks 🤫, helping you level up and maximize your rewards over time. Detailed information coming soon!

## Transparency

Transparency through the Blockchain: Results will be publicly verifiable on-chain and on Silencio's website shortly after the draw.

## Rules

*   100 million prizes are distributed across categories (Iron, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Amethyst, Sapphire, Emerald, Ruby, and Diamond). The categories reflect different $SLC values per prize; any user can win any prize, regardless of their league. The more raffle tickets they collect, the higher the chances of winning.
*   OG Bonus: Users receive an additional 20% bonus on all Coins or tickets earned over the past 12 months (12 months before the current raffle month). This incentivizes consistent and active participation from our users. The OG Bonus can be further enhanced by staking raffle rewards within the app.
*   Monthly reset: The raffle resets on the first day of each month at 00:00 GMT+1 and ends on the last day at 23:59 GMT+1.
*   Rewards: On the last day of each month at 23:59 GMT+1, a snapshot of each user's current number of raffle tickets will be taken. This snapshot will determine the user's probability of receiving $SLC token prizes in that month's raffle. This is subject to change.
*   Every user has the opportunity to win any prize, regardless of their league.
*   Transparency through the Blockchain: Results will be publicly verifiable on-chain and on Silencio's website shortly after the draw.
*   Claiming prizes: Once prizes become available, users will have 21 days to claim their $SLC winnings via the mobile app. Any unclaimed $SLC after 21 days will be reallocated to the future community rewards pool. For example, if prizes become claimable on the 3rd of the month, users will have until the 24th to claim rewards.
*   Payments: 541,666,666.67 $SLC will be available for claiming starting from the first three days following each monthly raffle.
*   Vesting and Lock-Up: There will be no lock-up period for $SLC tokens rewarded through the raffle system. However, a 30-day linear daily vesting schedule will apply from when the user claims the tokens.
*   Eligibility: Participation in the monthly raffle is not guaranteed solely by engaging in measuring activities. Eligibility to receive tokens is subject to your country's regulatory requirements. If your county's regulations do not permit participation in token airdrops, you will not be eligible to receive $SLC tokens. Please ensure compliance with your local laws before participating.
*   Fairness: Users caught cheating will be permanently deleted from the network. The community has the right to report any suspicious activity anonymously through the Silencio App, and the Silencio team will make the final decision regarding user deletion.
*   Staking: Users will have the opportunity to stake $SLC earnings obtained through the monthly raffle system. In return, they will significantly increase their chances of winning in future raffle draws. (More information coming soon)

The number of individual prizes, the value of each prize, and the historic base raffle bonus are subject to change as the network evolves. Of the total monthly designated amount of 541,666,666.67 $SLC tokens, 100% will be distributed per the emission schedule regardless of changes in the reward distribution. Users will be informed at least one month prior in case of any changes to the reward distribution.
