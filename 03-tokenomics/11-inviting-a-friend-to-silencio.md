# Inviting a Friend to Silencio

**Users who invite a friend to the network will receive a continuous 20% bonus on the friend's in-app Coin rewards.**

**For the Beta Airdrop only:** this extends to a 20% share of the friend's beta airdrop $SLC token earnings. In addition, users will give 20% of their beta airdrop $SLC token earnings to the friend who invited them. If users sign up without an invite code, their 20% beta airdrop $SLC token share will return to the pool for future community rewards.
