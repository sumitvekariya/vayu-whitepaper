# Staking

**Maximize your raffle rewards by staking your $SLC.**

Staking boosts your OG bonus for future raffles, **increasing your chances of winning a larger share of the 541 Million $SLC available each month in the form of 100 million unique monthly prizes.** It also unlocks exclusive perks 🤫

## How does it work?

1.  **Claim Your Airdrop:** Receive your $SLC airdrop rewards.
2.  **Start Staking:** Choose the percentage of your airdrop to stake (25%, 50%, 75%, or 100%) and select a staking duration (3, 6, 9, 12, 15, or 18 months).
3.  **Confirm & Reap Rewards:** Instantly see your increased OG Bonus reflected and confirm your stake. Significantly boost your chances of winning epic prizes in upcoming raffles.

### **Staking Benefits**

*   **Massive Raffle Boosts:** Significantly increase your winning odds in future raffles.
*   **Extra Prizes:** Increase your chances of winning epic monthly prizes. Check our socials for details.
*   **Stake Your Unvested Tokens:** Gain direct value from your unvested tokens.

## Rules

*   **OG Bonus:**
    *   The OG Bonus provides one raffle ticket for each in-app coin a user earns within 12 months before the raffle month. This excludes the current raffle period.
    *   Users always start with a base OG Bonus of 20%.
*   **Staking Bonus:**
    *   The Staking Bonus increases your OG Bonus for the duration of your stake.
    *   Example: If you stake for 18 months, the bonus will be active for those 18 months.
    *   Available Stake Percentages: 25%, 50%, 75%, and 100% of your last airdrop.
    *   Available Staking Durations: 3, 6, 9, 12, 15, and 18 months.
    *   The actual Staking Bonus is calculated based on the chosen percentage and duration.

*   **Staking Bonus Calculation:**
    *   Staking bonuses are applied equally to all users based on the chosen percentage, regardless of the amount of $SLC allocated, ensuring fairness.
    *   Bonuses from multiple stakes are additive.
        *   Example: 20% base OG bonus + 30% bonus in month 1 + 5% bonus in month 2 = 55% total bonus.
    *   Bonuses are only active during the respective staking period.
*   **Staking Eligibility:**
    *   Single Stake per Airdrop: Staking is only possible once per airdrop.
    *   The staking window for each airdrop is 21 days after the airdrop is available to claim.
    *   Example: If you claim an airdrop on the 10th of the month, and the airdrop was available for claiming starting on the 1st of that month, you can continue staking until the 21st of the same month.
*   **Staking Limits:**
    *   The maximum stake amount is 100% of your latest airdrop received.
    *   Example: If your last airdrop was 1,000 $SLC, you can only stake a maximum of 1,000 $SLC.
*   **Other:**
    *   **IMPORTANT: Staked tokens are inaccessible for the duration of the staking period and cannot be unstaked.**
    *   Future staking bonuses and OG bonuses are subject to change, but these changes will not be applied retroactively.
