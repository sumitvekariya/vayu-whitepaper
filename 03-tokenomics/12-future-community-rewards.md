# Future Community Rewards

This reward pool will serve as the primary source of community rewards after the prescribed token issuance period. It collects the following:

1.  $SLC tokens from unclaimed community token monthly distributions
2.  $SLC token participation in network commercial initiatives
3.  A 20% share of beta airdrop $SLC token earnings will be collected from users who did not use an invite code when signing up (for more information, see the Referral Program section). The 20% share of Beta Airdrop $SLC token earnings from users who used an invite code will go directly to the individual who invited them to the network.
