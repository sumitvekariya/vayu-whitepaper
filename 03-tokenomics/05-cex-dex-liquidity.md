# CEX & DEX Liquidity

To ensure market liquidity and stability, 7.0 billion $SLC tokens will be allocated to provide the necessary liquidity of the $SLC token. The Silencio Network has appointed the BlockSound Foundation to manage the liquidity pool. After the transition to a token governance model, this decision can be overridden by a majority vote of $SLC token holders. Tokens in this pool are non-voting.
