# Beta Airdrop

During the beta phase, 7.5 billion $SLC tokens will be allocated to reward users who contribute data to the network. This reward mechanism is designed to recognize users who participate and help build the network before a detailed reward system is established.

At the Token Generation Event (TGE), 50% of the beta airdrop tokens will be released. The remaining 50% will be linearly vested over six months (When the user claims his airdrop). After the TGE, users will have 21 days (3 weeks) to claim their beta airdrop reward. If they miss this window, the unclaimed tokens will revert to the future community rewards pool for future distributions.

Each user's share of beta airdrop tokens will be determined by their contributions to the network, based on the number of in-app Coins they have directly or indirectly generated.

Silencio ensures that every active member receives a share of the rewards. The airdrop distribution will be divided into ten dynamic leagues, each containing 10% of the network's users. The higher a user's league, the greater the rewards. League rewards are distributed equally to all users within that league and are paid in addition to the Diamond Hands bonus. The distribution is as follows:

| League | % of Users | Reward per User |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Diamond | Top 10% | 2.5x |
| Platinum | 10% | 2.0x |
| Gold | 10% | 1.5x |
| Silver | 10% | 1.25x |
| Bronze | 10% | 1.0x |
| Iron | 10% | 0.75x |
| Copper | 10% | 0.5x |
| Tin | 10% | 0.25x |
| Lead | 10% | 0.1x |
| Stone | Bottom 10% | 0.05x |

The top 25,000 users at the time of the TGE will receive a substantial part of the total beta airdrop to ensure that Silencio's core users are fairly compensated for their prior work. These $SLC and regular league rewards will be distributed equally to all eligible users in this category.

Finally, a Lucky Silencian Bonus will be randomly distributed equally among 1,000 lucky users. To qualify, users must have a minimum balance of 10,000 in-app Coins (excluding referral Coins) on the day of the airdrop.
