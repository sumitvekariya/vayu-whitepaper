# Initial Community Campaigns

As the cornerstone of DePIN, the community is at the core of Silencio's mission. The Initial Community Campaigns bucket is specifically designated to fuel network growth and enhance incentives for the community raffle system. 11.00 B $SLC tokens have been reserved for these initial campaigns.

The BlockSound Token Co offers its community early access to the SLC token through a community public sale to honor this commitment. This initiative ensures that core contributors can acquire tokens before the Token Generation Event (TGE) at a preferential rate. The allocation of $SLC tokens for the public sale will be finalized and communicated ahead of TGE.
