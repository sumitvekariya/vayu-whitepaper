# Core Contributors

See the Team section to learn more about the team responsible for building the technical and operational systems that power the Silencio Network. They will receive 14.0 billion $SLC tokens in return for their hard work.

## Core contributors are responsible for:

*   Developing and continuously improving the mobile application
*   Providing and managing the data storage needed to operate the network
*   Developing and operating the Web2 & Web3 infrastructure and reward distribution mechanism
*   Monetizing data collected through B2B and B2C services
*   Promoting the network and the project growth
*   Providing end-user support and managing community platforms such as Discord servers, social media, and events

In addition, the network grants the Silencio Company an exclusive license to sell network data and services to third parties.

3.00 B tokens have been reserved for advisors of the Silencio Network.

Tokens allocated to core contributors and advisors are subject to a vesting period.
