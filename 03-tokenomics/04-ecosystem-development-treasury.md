# Ecosystem Development Treasury

An ecosystem development treasury is dedicated to ensuring the project's sustainability, growth, and governance, with 14.0 billion $SLC allocated for this purpose. These funds are divided between the general and expansion reserves to support project development, technological advancements, and scalability. Additionally, the treasury facilitates ecosystem growth by providing grants, forming partnerships, and offering community incentives to cultivate an engaged and dynamic user base.

Tokens in this pool are non-voting.
